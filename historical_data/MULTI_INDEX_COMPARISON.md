## Market Characteristics That Favor DCA

### ‚úÖ Strong DCA Candidates:

1. **High Volatility Markets** (NASDAQ)
   - Large swings create buying opportunities
   - DCA averages out extreme prices

2. **Declining/Sideways Markets** (Nikkei)
   - Prolonged bear markets
   - DCA continuously buys cheaper

3. **Bubble-Prone Markets** (Tech stocks)
   - Extreme peaks followed by crashes
   - DCA avoids buying only at peak

4. **Emerging/Unstable Markets**
   - High uncertainty
   - DCA reduces entry point risk

### ‚ùå Weak DCA Candidates:

1. **Stable Growth Markets** (S&P 500 long-term)
   - Consistent upward trend
   - Lump-sum captures full compounding

2. **Low Volatility Markets** (Blue chips)
   - Less benefit from averaging
   - Opportunity cost of delayed investment

3. **Post-Crash Markets**
   - Already at bottom
   - Lump-sum better positioned
