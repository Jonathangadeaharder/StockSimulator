## üí° Strategic Insights

### When to Use **Lump-Sum 2x Leverage**:

‚úÖ High confidence in multi-year bull market
‚úÖ Low volatility environment
‚úÖ Post-crash recovery (buying opportunity)
‚úÖ Stable markets (S&P 500, DJIA)
‚úÖ 15+ year horizon in developed markets

**Expected win rate**: 70-95% depending on market/timeframe

**Risk**: Catastrophic losses if market crashes immediately (-36% to -94%)

---

### When to Use **Monthly $500 ‚Üí 2x Leverage**:

‚úÖ Want leverage upside but can't time market
‚úÖ Expect market crash mid-period (allows buying dips)
‚úÖ Very high volatility expected

**Expected win rate**: 4-17% (very low!)

**Reality check**: Usually squeezed by both lump-sum and non-leveraged. Only makes sense if you can't invest lump-sum AND want leverage exposure.

### When to Use **Monthly $500 ‚Üí Non-Leveraged**:

‚úÖ Risk-averse investor (can't stomach 70-90% drawdowns)
‚úÖ Structural decline possible (Nikkei-like markets)
‚úÖ High uncertainty about market direction
‚úÖ Emerging markets or unstable economies
‚úÖ Need capital preservation over returns
‚úÖ Possible bubble environment (dot-com 2000, Japan 1989)

**Expected win rate**: 0-22% depending on market

**Key value**: **Insurance against disaster** - wins rarely but saves you in worst cases
