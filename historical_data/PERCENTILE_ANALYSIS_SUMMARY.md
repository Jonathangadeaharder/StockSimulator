**Key Question**: What returns can you realistically expect at different probability levels?

---

## üìä Percentile Interpretation Guide

- **10th percentile**: Only 10% of outcomes were worse (90% were better)
- **25th percentile**: Only 25% of outcomes were worse (75% were better)
- **50th percentile (median)**: Half of outcomes were better, half were worse
- **75th percentile**: 75% of outcomes were worse (only 25% were better)
- **90th percentile**: 90% of outcomes were worse (only top 10% achieved this)

**Example**: If 10th percentile return is -5%, this means 90% of historical periods did better than -5%.
